Our team
We are Ana, Diana and Ilinca and together we created the MicroCosmos application.
I, Ana, took care of the biology part, with the help of our teacher. I created a database in Supabase, then I took pictures of microscopic organisms, using a microscope and a specialized camera. After that, I added the pictures to the database, along with the information about what we see in the images.
I, Diana, created the application, its functionality and design. I created all these things in the FlutterFlow platform. With the help of the database created by Ana, I entered the information and pictures into the application.
I, Ilinca, created the application presentation site, in HTML, in Visual Studio Code. With the help of the information provided by Ana and Diana, I managed to create a summary of the application, so that it would be easier for users to use and understand the role of the application.
